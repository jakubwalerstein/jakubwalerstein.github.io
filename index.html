    • Jakub's House Samples

        These sounds came from a recording session at my house in Carmel, IN. The most notable sounds in this pack are the decays, which were created by playings horns (saxophone, traditional shofar) into the body of a piano while the sustain pedal was held. This allowed the piano strings to resonate with the horn, creating an overtone-rich decay. These sounds were recorded using a mid-side microphone configuration.
